LUIZ CHAMON

A scientist that pretends (every so often convincingly) to be a multi-instrumentalist songwriter, Luiz Chamon targets an eclectic pop sound, going from modern genres to folk rhythms from Brazil and Greece.

Luiz started hitting his fingers on the keyboard almost as early as he started taking things apart. This shared interest in music and engineering is what drove him to start working in recording studios and theater productions. It is also the reason he went on to pursue a career doing research in signal processing, a work that has led him to spend time in France, the USA, and Greece. These experiences fueled his love for learning new languages and cultures, especially through music, a love that inspired his debut solo EP "Philathina," a mix of American pop ballads, French musical storytelling tradition, and Greek themes.

One the best things about writing music is hearing back from listeners. Reach out to share your thoughts or just say "Hi!" by email or on Instagram.
